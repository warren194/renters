Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Question 1: What is this webapp for?
    Answer: This webapp is for renting out school items.
  2. Question 2: How do I rent an item?
    Answer: Click on the “select item” button and choose the item, you would like to rent, and proceed to rent the item and checkout.
  3. Question 3: Can I pay using my PayPal account?
    Answer: Yes, you can pay using your PayPal account or your debit card. You just have to select your means of payment.
